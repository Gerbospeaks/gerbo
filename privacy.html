Last updated on 25th July 2025

At Gerbo, we’re committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under SA data protection law.

1. Who we are

Gerbo is an AI-powered call answering service designed to help small businesses capture more leads and reduce missed opportunities. Our service is operated by [Insert Company Name], registered in the UK.

If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at:
📧 support@gerbospeaks.com

2. What data we collect

When you visit our website:

• IP address and browser type (for analytics and security)

• Device information

• Pages visited and time spent (via cookies)

When you use our demo or sign up:

• Your name and business details

• Contact details (email and/or phone number)

• Call recordings or summaries generated during demos

When your customers call via Gerbo:

• Caller name (if given)

• Caller phone number

• Call content and intent summary

Note: We only process this data to provide you with the Gerbo service and do not sell it to third parties.
